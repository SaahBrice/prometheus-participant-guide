🌱 EcoQuest Adventures

EcoQuest Adventures is an AI-powered educational game that helps children develop positive everyday habits through immersive storytelling, interactive missions, and gamified learning.

Instead of simply reading about environmental protection or healthy living, children actively practice these concepts by completing engaging missions, solving challenges, and answering quizzes. Every activity is designed to transform knowledge into lasting habits.

🎯 Problem

Many educational programs teach children what they should do, but few help them consistently practice those behaviors. As a result, important lessons about sustainability, hygiene, and responsible citizenship are often forgotten.

💡 Our Solution

EcoQuest Adventures turns learning into an adventure.

Players become Eco Rangers who explore different worlds, complete educational missions, interact with Sprout, an AI learning companion, and earn rewards as they progress.

The platform currently focuses on:

🌍 Environmental awareness

🧼 Hygiene and healthy habits

♻️ Responsible waste management

🌱 Sustainable everyday actions


Because the platform is modular, new educational themes such as nutrition, road safety, digital citizenship, and financial literacy can easily be added.

🚀 Vision

EcoQuest Adventures is more than an educational game.

It is a scalable learning platform designed to help children build lifelong habits through play. By combining gamification, storytelling, and AI-powered guidance, we aim to make learning engaging, accessible, and impactful for schools, families, NGOs, and communities worldwide.

> We don't just teach children what is right—we help them turn knowledge into lifelong habits. 🌱
